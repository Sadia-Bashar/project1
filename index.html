The Rose

The rose is the most beautiful and loveliest flower.
She is called the queen of all flowers. It presents a beautiful
sight and makes our house more beautiful. They are of many colours and sizes.
Some are big and some are small.S A rose has a number of soft petals. They are of many colours. Such as white, black,
red, rosy etc. It is a symbol of love. It is a symbol of beauty.
It has a sweet smell. People of all classes like it. We get Ator and
rose water from it. We also use it on our happy occasions.
